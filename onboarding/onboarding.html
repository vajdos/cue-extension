Cue

Welcome to Cue.

A real-time conversation intelligence layer for your video calls. Cue listens to how you speak — pace, tension, energy — and nudges you when it's time to slow down, breathe, or give the other person space.

It does not record, transcribe, or send audio anywhere. Everything runs on your device.

What you'll see on a call.

Cue joins any Teams, Zoom, or Meet call and shows a small widget in the corner.

≡
Three live bars
Tension, pace, and energy — all relative to your own baseline. The widget calibrates to you in the first 20 seconds.
·
Subtle first-touch nudges
The first time anything needs your attention, you'll see a faint glow on the screen edge. No text. No interruption.
!
Escalates only if ignored
If you don't respond — keep talking fast, keep tension high — Cue steps up gradually. It never starts at "loud."
⤓
Integration Tape at the end
When your call ends, a timeline opens showing your EQ score and every moment Cue flagged — so you can review what happened.
Using the Teams desktop app? Cue can't reach inside Microsoft's native Teams app (Chrome extensions don't run there). There's a 5-minute, one-time setup that gives you the same look-and-feel in a window Cue can see — open the setup guide.

Pick your nudge style.

How do you want Cue to talk to you? You can change this anytime from the extension popup.

Your data never leaves.

•
Nothing is recorded. Cue analyzes audio features (pace, tension, energy) in real time and discards the raw audio immediately.
•
Nothing leaves your browser. All session data is stored locally in IndexedDB. No servers, no transcripts, no uploads.
•
You control the mic. Cue only starts when you click Start on the widget. Stop it anytime.
•
On-device voice vocabulary is opt-in. If you turn it on from the popup, Cue learns the phrases you use so nudges sound like you. Transcription runs entirely in your browser — nothing uploaded. Off by default.

Don’t take our word for it
You can verify the privacy claim yourself in 30 seconds.
  1. Press F12 in any tab where Cue is running → click the Network tab.
  2. Start a coaching session. The Network tab will show zero outgoing requests from the extension during the session.
  3. Open the built-in privacy verifier any time: Cue → Verify privacy.
